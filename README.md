## What is PathAI?

PathAI is an AI-powered personalized learning system that analyzes how a student actually performed — not just what score they got — and builds a fully customized study roadmap around their exact strengths and weaknesses.

No generic content. No guesswork. Just a plan built for *you*.

---

## 🔄 How It Works

```
📸 Upload marksheet / Enter scores manually
           ↓
🤖 Vision AI extracts subject, topics, scores & mistake patterns
           ↓
🧠 LLM analyzes performance — finds weak areas, ranks priority
           ↓
📊 Dashboard: 3D chart + resources + practice questions + 7-day plan
           ↓
💬 AI Tutor chatbot answers doubts with your context already loaded
           ↓
🔄 Retake test → AI compares old vs new → Updated plan generated
           ↓
📧 Daily email reminder lands every morning with today's tasks
```

Everything happens in under 10 seconds. No manual work after the first upload.

## ✨ Features & How They Work

**📸 Snap & Analyze**
Upload or photograph any marksheet or answer sheet. A vision AI model reads the image, extracts subject, topic-wise scores, and mistake patterns — completely automatically. No typing, no forms, just a photo.

**🧠 Deep Performance Analysis**
The AI doesn't just look at your total score. It breaks down every topic individually, figures out where exactly you're losing marks, and sorts your weak areas by priority — so you always know what needs attention first, not just what you failed.

**🗺️ Personalized 7-Day Study Plan**
Based on your weak areas and how many hours you can study per day, PathAI generates a specific day-by-day plan. Day 1 focuses on your weakest topic, Day 7 is a full review — with exact tasks listed for each day, not vague suggestions.

**📚 Curated Resource Recommendations**
For each weak topic, PathAI recommends specific videos, articles, and practice sets — with a direct search link. Resources are categorized by type so you can filter what format works best for you.

**❓ AI-Generated Practice Questions**
Custom questions targeting your exact weak topics — sorted easy to hard. Each question comes with a hint so you can think through it without getting the answer handed to you.

**🔄 Adaptive Re-Analysis**
Retake a test, enter your new scores, and PathAI compares them against your previous attempt — showing which topics improved, which still need work, and generating a completely updated plan based on where you are *now*.

**💬 Context-Aware AI Tutor**
The built-in chatbot already knows your subject, weak areas, and mistake patterns from your analysis. Every answer is filtered through that context — it doesn't explain Algebra when you need help with Geometry.

**📊 3D Performance Visualization**
Topic-wise scores rendered as an interactive 3D radar chart — auto-rotating, hoverable, color-coded by performance. Weak topics glow red, strong ones green. Drag and explore from any angle.

**📧 Automated Daily Reminders**
After analysis, PathAI sends a welcome email with your full plan. Every morning after that, an automated email arrives with today's focus topic and exactly what to do.

Most edtech apps give everyone the same content.

PathAI starts with *your* data — your scores, your mistakes, your time — and builds everything around that. The AI doesn't just recommend; it reasons about why you're struggling and what the most efficient path forward looks like.

The goal: a system that works like a personal tutor who has seen your paper, knows your weak spots, and has your next week planned before you even ask.
